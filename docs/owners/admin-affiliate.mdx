## What this section controls

| Control | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Trading fee (%)** | Sets platform trading fee charged per execution. |
| **Affiliate share (%)** | Sets what portion of trading fee is paid to affiliates. |
| **Totals cards** | Shows referrals, referred volume, and affiliate fees. |

---

## Important constraints

- Trading fee has a minimum enforced by onchain base fee.
- Affiliate share is applied to trading fee, not directly to traded volume.

---

## Operational routine

1. Adjust fee/share before campaign launches.
2. Track totals after major traffic pushes.
3. Rebalance fee economics if affiliate payouts outpace strategy.
