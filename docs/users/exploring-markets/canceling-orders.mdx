## How to Cancel Orders

You can cancel any open limit order before it is matched.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open your open orders

    Navigate to <strong>My Account → Open Orders</strong> to review all active
    limit orders waiting to fill.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Select the order

    Locate the order marked <strong>Open</strong> that you want to cancel and
    expand the row for details.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Cancel and confirm

    Press <strong>Cancel Order</strong> to release the reserved funds back to
    your available balance immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
