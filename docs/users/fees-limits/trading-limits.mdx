## Trading thresholds (current app behavior)

- Market buy minimum: <strong>$1.00</strong>
- Limit order minimum size: <strong>0.01 shares</strong>
- Buy orders cannot exceed available balance
- Sell orders cannot exceed available shares

## Deposits and withdrawals

Limits depend on selected funding route/provider (wallet bridge, card on-ramp, or transfer flow).
Always review the transfer modal before confirming.

## Export limits

CSV export is available from <strong>/portfolio → History</strong> and exports the rows currently visible after filters.
