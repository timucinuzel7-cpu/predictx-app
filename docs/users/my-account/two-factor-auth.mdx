## Enable 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the Two-Factor screen

    Visit <strong>My Account → Two-Factor Auth</strong> to start the setup
    flow.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Scan and verify

    Scan the QR code with your authenticator app and type the 6-digit code to
    confirm the pairing.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Save backup codes

    Download or copy the backup codes and store them securely for account
    recovery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
If you lose access to your 2FA device and backup codes, account recovery may take longer and require additional verification.
</Callout>
