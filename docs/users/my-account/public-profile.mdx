## What others can see

Your public profile includes high-level portfolio stats and recent activity.

Common elements:

- Username and avatar
- Joined date
- Portfolio performance cards
- Public positions/activity tabs (depending on your visibility settings)

## Profile URL

Your profile is accessible by handle at <strong>/@username</strong>.
You can open it directly from <strong>Settings → View Public Profile</strong>.
