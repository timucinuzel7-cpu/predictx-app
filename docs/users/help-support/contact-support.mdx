## Support Channels

There are several ways to get help while using <SiteName />:

- **Discord community** – fastest way to get peer-to-peer help and updates
- **FAQ page** – quick answers to common questions
- **Email support** – for account-specific issues (response times vary)

---

## Support Hours

- Community channels: **24/7** (responses depend on community activity)
- Direct support: typically answered during **business hours** (Monday–Friday)

---

## Escalation

If your issue is not resolved through normal support:

1. Open a ticket via Discord or email
2. Include screenshots, transaction IDs, and a clear description of the problem
3. The support team may escalate to technical or compliance review if necessary
